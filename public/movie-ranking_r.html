Top Movies of All Time According to Rodica

My best 3 movies.

Seven Years in Tibet

The emotional and spiritual true story about the friedship between Heinrich Harrer, an Austrian mountain climber and Dalai Lama, at the time of China's takeover of Tibet.

Scent of a Woman

A motivational and impressive message about life and relationships between different generations.

Once Upon a Time in America

An impressive story about America in the 1920s.
